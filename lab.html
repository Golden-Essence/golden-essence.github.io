Our Cultivation Methodology

From spore to harvest, our controlled environment guarantees maximum bioactive compounds like Cordycepin and Adenosine.

1. Organic Substrate Preparation

We use a proprietary, 100% vegan organic substrate—free from insects—ensuring a clean, ethical, and highly nutritious base for our fungi to thrive.

2. Sterile Inoculation

Working under strict Class 100 Laminar Flow Hoods, our technicians inoculate the substrate with premium Cordyceps cultures to prevent any cross-contamination.

3. Climate-Controlled Incubation

Our intelligent climate systems mimic the harsh, high-altitude conditions of the Himalayas, triggering the mushrooms to produce peak levels of active compounds.

Uncompromising Quality Control

  • HEPA Filtration: Hospital-grade air filtration ensures a 99.99% sterile breathing environment for the growing fungi.
  • LED Light Spectrum Tuning: Customized lighting cycles optimize the growth and pigmentation of the Cordyceps fruiting bodies.
  • Third-Party Testing: Every harvest is rigorously tested for heavy metals, microbial impurities, and active compound potency.
